ABOUT

Web Design! It all started when I was around 13 years old when Blogger was a thing? There was an amazing Japanese themes designer that I was always impressed by his work. So, from time to time, I would go through his templates code, try to understand it, and apply some of his cool effects to my Blog. After that, I learned from a friend about WordPress. I used it for years for some personal projects. And now, I use HTML, CSS, and a bit of JS.

3D Design! My interest started when I was a student at the Specialized Institute of Applied Technology. since I got a building designer diploma and a structural work diploma. it made me interested in applying what I learned in 3D apps, so I learned how to use 3ds max then I came across Andrew Price's " The Donut Guy " channel and got the chance to know about Blender thanks to him.

Digital Art, Drawing, Character Design? I started drawing as 5 years old kid. Back in primary school, I wasn't that cool kid but what kept me being seen as an amazing guy was my pen. I started learning about character design, pixel art, and all that stuff through Udemy courses and I applied the knowledge I gained in some personal projects.

Lastly I hope to expand my graphic design either 2D or 3D career and gain more knowledge.
